# Survey Drafts — Full Page Screenshot

Ready-to-use question structures for Tally.so or Google Forms.

---

## Feedback Survey (link from popup)

Linked from the "Send feedback" text in the popup UI.

### Questions

1. **How do you use Full Page Screenshot?** (Multiple choice, select all)
   - Web development / QA testing
   - Content creation / Social media
   - Research / Documentation
   - Bug reporting
   - Other (free text)

2. **How often do you use it?** (Single choice)
   - Daily
   - A few times a week
   - A few times a month
   - I just tried it once

3. **What would you improve?** (Free text, optional)
   - Placeholder: "e.g., faster capture, better quality, keyboard shortcut..."

4. **Overall rating** (1-5 stars)
   - 1 = Not useful → 5 = Essential tool

---

## Uninstall Survey (chrome.runtime.setUninstallURL)

Shown automatically when user removes the extension.

### Questions

1. **Why are you uninstalling?** (Single choice)
   - It doesn't work on a website I need
   - Too slow
   - Found a better alternative
   - I don't need screenshot tool anymore
   - Privacy concerns
   - Other (free text)

2. **Which website didn't work?** (Free text, optional)
   - Placeholder: "e.g., gmail.com, notion.so..."

3. **Which alternative are you switching to?** (Free text, optional, shown if "Found a better alternative")
   - Placeholder: "e.g., GoFullPage, Fireshot..."

---

## Notes

- Keep surveys short (under 2 minutes to complete)
- Tally.so recommended: free, no branding, embeddable, GDPR-compliant
- Uninstall survey should be even shorter — users are leaving, they won't fill long forms
