Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Full Page Screenshot collects zero personal data. Everything stays on your device.

What This Extension Does

Full Page Screenshot captures screenshots of web pages using a scroll-and-stitch technique. Screenshots are saved directly to your computer via Chrome's built-in download API. No data ever leaves your browser.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No browsing history is recorded
  • No screenshots are uploaded anywhere
  • No analytics or tracking scripts are included
  • No cookies are set
  • No network requests are made to external servers

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab — Access the current tab to capture its visible content. Only activated when you click the extension button.
  • scripting — Inject a content script that scrolls the page, measures its dimensions, and detects scrollable containers. Required for the scroll-and-stitch technique.
  • downloads — Save the final screenshot PNG to your computer using Chrome's download API.
  • webNavigation — Detect iframe frames within the current page so the extension can capture content inside iframes.
  • host_permissions: <all_urls> — Required for content script injection into pages with cross-origin iframes (e.g., embedded widgets). Without this, the extension cannot scroll and measure those frames.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms.

Data Storage

No data is stored by the extension. Screenshots are saved to your local filesystem via Chrome's download manager, using the location you configured in Chrome settings.

Children's Privacy

This extension does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the update date at the top will reflect the change. Since this extension collects no data, significant policy changes are unlikely.
